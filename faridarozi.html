Memory

When i was in 8th class

When I was in the 8th grade, I participated in a competition, when the results of the competition were announced, everyone called my name as encouragement, and this was a great honor for me, and I won that competition.

What is my big dream

My great wish is to see Afghanistan in peace and prosperity. It will be a day when the people of my land can live without fear. It will be a day when all the immigrants will return to their homeland and there will be the voice of peace in every street of Afghanistan. Girls will be able to study without fear and women's rights and Men are equal

What is my big dream 2

The day I can return to my country, I can serve my land and my people, the day I can achieve my dream of becoming a journalist.
